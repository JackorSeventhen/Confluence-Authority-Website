EVERGLOW SYSTEM OVERVIEW

The Everglow System represents humanity's most ambitious interstellar colonization project to date. Orbiting the G7V star "Everglow," this system of six planets was first identified by the CONFLUENCE CHARTING ORBITAL OBSERVATORIES (CCOO) in 2722, offering unprecedented opportunities for scientific research, resource exploitation, and permanent settlement.
