Human Characters
From the legendary Aegis II Ascendants defending humanity's interests across the stars, to the determined Free Reach rebels fighting against Confluence oppression, and the enigmatic Primarch Council pulling strings from the shadows - discover the complex web of human characters whose actions will determine the fate of our species. Alien Characters

From the zealous Krell to the nomadic Vokri, each species brings its own legends to this cosmic conflict.
